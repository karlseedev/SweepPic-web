1. Service Overview

SweepPic (the "App") is an app that helps organize photos on iOS devices. It provides features such as swipe-to-delete, similar photo analysis, and auto cleanup.

2. Definitions

  • Trash: A temporary storage area within the App. Photos marked for deletion are held here and can be recovered until "Empty" is performed.
  • Empty: The action of moving photos from the Trash to the iOS system trash ("Recently Deleted").
  • SweepPic Pro: A monthly or annual auto-renewing subscription product.
