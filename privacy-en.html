SweepPic Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026 | Effective: March 11, 2026

1. Overview

SweepPic (the "App") is an iOS app specialized in photo organization. Photo data and face recognition results are processed only on the device and are never transmitted to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

The App automatically collects the following data for service improvement:

a) TelemetryDeck (Analytics)

TelemetryDeck applies identifier protection measures such as double hashing, so that original identifiers are not stored as-is.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • Improving app features and diagnosing errors
  • Enhancing service quality through usage pattern analysis
  • Displaying advertisements (Google AdMob)
  • Verifying subscription status and providing paid features
  • Processing invite codes, referral rewards, and preventing abuse
  • Sending referral reward and app-related notifications

4. Data Retention Period

  • TelemetryDeck analytics data: Subject to TelemetryDeck's data retention policy
  • Supabase analytics events: Retained for 365 days from collection, then automatically deleted
  • Referral program user ID and referral/reward records: Retained as long as necessary for referral services, reward processing, abuse prevention, and customer support
  • Push notification tokens: Retained as long as necessary for notification delivery; updated or deleted upon permission revocation or token renewal
  • App data on device is deleted upon app removal. However, the referral program user ID stored in Keychain may persist after reinstallation per iOS policy

7. Face Recognition Technology

The App uses Apple's Vision Framework to automatically detect and zoom into faces in photos.

  • All processing is performed on-device only
  • Face data is neither stored nor transmitted externally
  • Not used for marketing, advertising, or data mining

8. Automated Decision-Making

The App's "Auto Cleanup" feature uses AI to analyze photo quality (exposure, blur, etc.) and suggest candidates for deletion.

  • No automatic deletion is performed; the final decision is always made by the user
  • Analysis results are processed on-device only

9. Behavioral Information

The App does not independently collect behavioral information for ad personalization. However, ads are displayed through Google AdMob, and Google's SDK may process related information for ad delivery and measurement. Without ATT consent, cross-app tracking-based personalized ads are not served.
